## Keen-Keeper

### About this project
This site helps you keep track of your friends and your connections. You can see how many friends you have, who you contact, and your interaction history.

### Key points
1. Track your friend list
2. Quick actions: Call, Text, Video
3. Timeline of your interactions
4. Simple stats with a pie chart
